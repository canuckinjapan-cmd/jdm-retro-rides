# JDM Retro Rides - Design Specifications

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Headings:** Plus Jakarta Sans, Bold/ExtraBold
- **Body Text:** Plus Jakarta Sans, Regular/Medium

## Color Palette
- **Text Color:** True Black (#000000) for all content text.
- **Exceptions:** Navigation links and interactive elements maintain their specific brand colors (Blue/Slate/White).
- **Primary Brand Color:** #0058be (Secondary in Tailwind config)
- **Background:** #f9f9f9 (Surface)
- **Surface Containers:** #eeeeee, #f3f3f4

## UI Elements
- **Border Radius:** 0.5rem (Default), 1rem (XL)
- **Navigation:** Glassmorphism effect (backdrop-blur)
- **Animations:** Reveal on scroll, Fade-in-up for hero section.
