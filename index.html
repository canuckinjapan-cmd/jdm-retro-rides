About Us

We have been exporting cars from Japan since 1996. We do not keep a lot of vehicles in stock and mainly buy to order but are happy to search for any particular model (we are members of most auction associations in Japan) and we can give you a rough idea of cost for any vehicle depending on the many variables.

All cars are supplied with auction report and service history. We organize all shipping and transportation thus saving you money. Our prices are very competitive and we aim to buy the best car for the best price. Drop us a line via the Contact form below.
